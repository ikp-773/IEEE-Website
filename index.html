About Us

The IEEE student branch of Mar Baselios College of Engineering & Technology was established on February 14, 2006 by Dr G Vijayaraghavan, former Chairperson of IEEE Kerala Section and Founder & CEO of Technopark, Trivandrum. IEEE MBCET falls under IEEE Kerala Section which is a part of the IEEE India Council which falls under Region 10 (Asia Pacific & Australia) of the global IEEE geography. We started our journey with 52 members and in 2008 it reached 103.

Society chapters are bodies that work for the advancements of one particular field of technology. We have the IEEE Computer Society student chapter integrated with our student branch to cater the advancements in the field of Computer Technologies.

Vision

“To be an Institution moulding globally competent Professionals as epitomes of Noble Values.”

Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology strives for excellence in higher education, research and support services, by employing an integral approach to education, with the purpose of forming technically competent engineers, responsible citizens and epitomes of fundamental human values like commitment, compassion and optimism, who ought to be actively involved in creative nation building and enhancement of human existence.
Mission

“To transform the Youth as technically competent, ethically sound and socially committed professionals, by providing a vibrant learning ambience, for the welfare of humanity.”

To transform young men and women into technically competent, socially committed and morally upright individuals who are well equipped for outstanding performance in the application of human resources and potentialities on a global dimension, and are empowered to serve humanity impelled by their unwavering commitment to the ultimate values of human existence.

What Next?

What Next? is an event conducted by IEEE SB MBCET, in association with the Career Guidance and Placement Unit MBCET, exclusively for the S7 students of MBCET, as a career guidance session. There could have been no better speakers other than our alumni for the session, who are currently employed in different domains. The students were made aware of the wide variety of opportunities open in front of them. Speakers - 1. Ajay Govind 2. Aravind Unnithan 3. Aksa Antony 4. Juby Number of Participants- 95

Genesis

EMBS Society of IEEE SB MBCET in collaboration with Biomedical Researcher Group conducted Genesis ,a three day webinar series. The inauguration of EMBS Society of IEEE SB MBCET was held on 10 th Aug 2020.The EMBS Code of Ethics which was delivered by Dr M J Jayashree miss ,followed by welcome speech and speech by VC .The inauguration was done by Sri Koruthu P Varghese sir (chairman ,IEEE EMBS Kerala Chapter) . Felicitations were done by Nandan sir and Vivek sir. Vote of thanks was given by Jinu sir First day of the webinar series was enlightened by Dr Sukesh Sir, He shared the keynotes on the ‘Trends of biomedical engg’. He explained on diagnostic equipments, application of computers in medicine etc. Second day ,the speaker was Amrutha C ,she explained on ‘Medical device development: concept of commercialization’ . she gave an overview on medical device development ,about the time they spend on research and testing .Last day, the speaker was Dr Godwinraj D, He gave a brief idea on ‘Low cost inkjet and PCB Technology for biomedical hardware implementation’.
