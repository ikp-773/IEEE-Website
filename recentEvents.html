IEEE Explore with Library

IEEE Xplore is a powerful resource for discovery and access to scientific and technical content published by the IEEE and its publishing partners. IEEE Xplore provides web access to more than five-million full text documents from some of the world’s most highly cited publications. IEEE MBCET SB conducted an online training on EBSCO eBooks and IEEE Xplore Digital Library from 11am to 12.30pm on 14 th of July. The webinar was open to all IEEE members. The session was handles by Mr. Nandu Lal T S, Senior Training Manager at EBSCO Information Services & IEEE. The webinar was conducted on the Cisco Webex meeting platform. There were a total of 85 participants. It was a really informative and interactive session where we got to know about many advantages of IEEE Xplore. There was also a fun quiz session and doubt clearance session at the end of the session. Topic: EBSCO eBooks and IEEE Xplore Digital Library Training for Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology Host: Nanda Lal TS Date: Tuesday, July 14, 2020 Time: 11:00 am, India Time (Mumbai, GMT+05:30)

Designing With Canva

WiE society of the IEEE MBCET SB conducted an online graphic designing workshop and poster designing competition Graphic Designing with Canva on 13 th of August at 5pm. Chassue Au Tresor

As a part of TechX conducted by the IEEE Travancore Hub, IEEE SB MBCET organized an online treasure hunt. The event saw participation from 14 different colleges of the Travancore Hub. The event commenced on 30th June 2020, with the clue to the first level dispatched at 10 am. The participants were given 12 hours to solve the clue. WiE Colloquium

WiE colloquium was a panel discussion series exclusively conducted by WiE AGs of various SBs. The primary purpose of the event was to ensure the updation of technological knowledge, thus the event focused on the latest technologies which are known as the pillars of the industries. All WiE AGs of Kerala section were divided into 9 groups and each group was given a topic. IEE MBCET SB was under team 4 along with MES college of Engineering (MES), Government college of Engineering-Kannur (GCEK), Musaliar college of Engineering Chirayinkeezhu (MESCE), Marian college of Engineering. Topic allotted for team 4 was Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT). The Industrial Internet of Things or IIoT is defined as “machines, computers and people enabling intelligent industrial operations using advanced data analytics for transformational business outcomes”. The Industrial Internet of Things takes the benefits of the Internet of Things in general to a higher level and also to the industries with high-stakes where human error could result in massive risks. The precision level that can be achieved through the IIoT is one of the greatest advantages that makes this discipline one of the most welcome gifts of IoT. IEEE LINK conducted this online panel of discussion on 3 rd of May from 8.00 pm to 9.00 pm. There were a total of 65 participants including IEEE members and Non IEEE members from our college and other colleges. Moderator for our team was Deepthi G A of MBCET, speakers were Ankitha Vinod of GCEK, Devika Muraleedaran of MESCE, Lavanya E V of GCEK, Sara Pius of MCE, Sarga Sarin of MCE, Shabeba K K of MESCE.

With Her

IEEE WIE is one of the world’s leaders in changing the face of engineering. The global network connects nearly 20,000 members in over 100 countries to advance women in technology at all points in their life and career. IEEE WIE members make lifelong friendships, acquire influential mentors, and make a difference for the benefit of humanity. WIE society of the IEEE MBCET SB conducted an online photography contest With Her from 17 th to 31 st of March. The contest was open to all. There were a total of 22 participants including IEEE members and Non IEEE members from our college and other colleges. Winners were selected on the basis of likes and the caption of their photo which were posted on the Instagram page of the IEEE MBCET SB. The winner of this event was Anakha Ajith of S2 EE1 of MBCET with 1974 likes.
