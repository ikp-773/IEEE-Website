Designing With Canva

WiE society of the IEEE MBCET SB conducted an online graphic designing workshop and poster designing competition Graphic Designing with Canva on 13 th of August at 5pm. The workshop and poster designing competition were open to all. There were a total of 56 participants including IEEE members and Non IEEE members from our college and other colleges. The session was handled by Arya.S.Potti, Chairperson WiE, IEEE MBCET SB. Themes for the poster designing competition were: 1. Covid19 - Redesign our life 2. EIA 2020: Boon or Bane? Participants were requested to choose a topic among the given two and were requested to submit the poster within 2 hours after the session. Judges declared the winners on the basis of clarity of topic, creativity and aesthetics of their poster that were submitted. Winners of the event were:  Sharan George Maamen of MBCET under the category: Covid19 - Redesign our life  Jishnu Narayanan of MBCET under the category: EIA 2020: Boon or Bane?

Chassue Au Tresor

As a part of TechX conducted by the IEEE Travancore Hub, IEEE SB MBCET organized an online treasure hunt. The event saw participation from 14 different colleges of the Travancore Hub. The event commenced on 30th June 2020, with the clue to the first level dispatched at 10 am. The participants were given 12 hours to solve the clue. Their responses were collected via mail, Whatsapp and Instagram messages. The participants who solved the clue of one level proceeded to the next. In the 5th and last level, the first contestant to solve the clue and find the treasure was considered as the winner. Kaveri T.S. from IEEE SB SCTCE secured the first prize and was awarded a cash prize of Rs 500/-

With Her

IEEE WIE is one of the world’s leaders in changing the face of engineering. The global network connects nearly 20,000 members in over 100 countries to advance women in technology at all points in their life and career. IEEE WIE members make lifelong friendships, acquire influential mentors, and make a difference for the benefit of humanity. WIE society of the IEEE MBCET SB conducted an online photography contest With Her from 17 th to 31 st of March. The contest was open to all. There were a total of 22 participants including IEEE members and Non IEEE members from our college and other colleges. Winners were selected on the basis of likes and the caption of their photo which were posted on the Instagram page of the IEEE MBCET SB. The winner of this event was Anakha Ajith of S2 EE1 of MBCET with 1974 likes.

Mentel Health

Technical Quiz

Technology is advancing faster than society can handle. It has been absorbed in everyone’s lives to the extent that every day we all interact with one kind of screen or another, mostly using internet. Therefore, awareness about the technological innovations has become a necessity. To test their knowledge, a technical quiz was organized by IEEE Computer Society as a pre-event of IEEE Kerala Section Students Technical Consortium- TECHSORTIUM 2020, On 28 th February ’20, as a part of Science week, a technical quiz was conducted at Shannon Hall from 1:00 pm - 2:00 pm for all interested students. The questions included MCQs and fill in the blanks on the subjects of recent advancements in technology, History, IEEE Standards etc. The competition had a participation of 41 students from all semesters. The winners selected were Sarang Dutt and Vaishnavy Menon of S4 CS2. The top winners selected will represent in the finals of Technical Quiz at Kerala’s largest technical consortium-TECHSORTIUM.

EMBS Quiz

Biomedical engineering is the application of engineering principles to the fields of biology and health care. This field seeks to close the gap between engineering and medicine, combining the design and problem solving skills of engineering with medical biological sciences to advance health care treatment, including diagnosis, monitoring, and therapy. The critical skills needed by a biomedical engineer include a well-rounded understanding of several areas of engineering as well as the specific area of application. The Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS) of the IEEE MBCET SB conducted a quiz on biomedical engineering on 20th February 2020, from 1:15 pm at Shannon Hall. It focused on the knowledge of the students in the field of biology and its application in engineering. The quiz was open to all the students. There were a total of 100 participants from among all the batches and branches. Participants were given 20 minutes to complete a set of questions comprising of 25 multiple choice questions. The winners of our prelims were Vivek P of S4 CE2 and Sania M.S. of S2 EC2 and they participated in the finals, held at LBS Institute for Women on 28th February 2020, as part of the National Science Day.

Python Coding Competition

IEEE MBCET in association with Developer Student Clubs MBCET hosted a Python Coding Competition at Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology. The event was hosted on 8th February 2020, with an aim to find the best programmers in Python programming language and was a sequel to the Python Coding Bootcamp conducted by Developer Student Clubs MBCET. The event started at 10:00 am at the Department Computing Facility of Electronics and Communication Engineering. The event was attended by 20 students, both offline and online. The students were provided with four programming challenges of increasing difficulty. The problems were hosted on an online challenge platform called HackerRank, using which the students solved and submitted the solutions for the challenges that they were provided with.
